OUR MISSION:

We share the Gospel of Jesus Christ in the context of installing sustainable water purification systems wherever they are needed.

We work to empower local ministry partners to bring physical, social and spiritual transformation to poor communities by providing sustainable, safe water solutions. Our vision is to see safe water provided in the name of Jesus in every poor community of the world.

We accomplish this by training local nationals to construct and operate clean water systems in churches, schools, and orphanages. We also train children, parents, and any interested parties in basic germ theory and personal hygiene.

CLEAN WATER

A purification system designed and developed to be a simple, low-cost unit easiely installed in remote locations.

Methods: Filtration -> Microfiltration -> Ozone Disinfection

Capacity: Based on Tank Size. Roughly 300 gallons/hour @ 5gpm per batch.

COST: $4250 (Operational Costs/100,000 gallons = ~$0.0075/gallon)

This cost includes the entire cost of the system, installation, and maintenance (Parts & Labor) for 3 years.

EDUCATION

As Jesus refreshes the spirit, so to does clean water refresh the body.

We teach germ theory and personal hygiene in conjunction with scripture with a focus on community and fun activities. Hygiene education covers proper handwashing techniques as well as concept such as 'grey' water.

Volunteers are always welcome to help teach the children or assist with the installation. No language skills needed!
