Simple Beginnings

Living Water World Missions (LWWM), founded in the summer of 2012, is a 501(c)(3) corporation made up of representatives from different churches in the Central Valley area of California. LWWM shares the Gospel of the Lord Jesus Christ in the context of installing sustainable water purification systems wherever they are needed.

Faith in Action

Living Water World Missions is a non-profit organization located in Visalia, California existing to share the Good News of Jesus Christ in the context of installing sustainable water purification systems for communities in need.

While our current ministry focus is in the Central American country of Guatemala, we are poised to install systems all around the world. We assist and train nationals in the building of clean water systems which use filters and ozone to remove germs and bacteria from city water sources, making it safe to consume. In addition, we train parents, children, and interested others in germ theory, hygiene, and the appropriate uses of clean water.

Typically the water systems are installed in churches, orphanages, schools or medical clinics, and are capable of providing sufficient clean drinking water for an entire community. In 2014 we began a partnership with Compassion International, a child sponsorship program, to install clean water systems in many of their sites in Guatemala.

We have already installed systems in other nations in the world including in Cuba, Haiti, Colombia, Honduras, Mexico, Ecuador, and Peru. We are committed to go where God leads us, and including on trips persons who wish to participate in this ministry with us.

What We Do

As of September 2022, LWWM has installed 95 water systems in Guatemala. In addition, LWWM has led other mission teams in installations in Mexico, Colombia, Ecuador, Peru, Haiti, and Cuba.

We install 300 gallon/hour water treatment systems for $4250. We go on to provide maintinance for 3 years guarenteed and have even taken over maintinance of other systems that use similar parts.

An installation trip to Guatemala will typically be one week in length, from Saturday to Saturday, and may cost up to $1500.00 per person (including airfare). Prices vary based on the accommodations and the cost of travel at the time the trips are booked.
