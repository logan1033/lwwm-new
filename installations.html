Simple Beginnings

Living Water World Missions (LWWM), founded in the summer of 2012, is a 501(c)(3) corporation made up of representatives from different churches in the Central Valley area of California. LWWM shares the Gospel of the Lord Jesus Christ in the context of installing sustainable water purification systems wherever they are needed.
