What do I need to be a team member?
LWWM allows God to decide who will go on a trip. In other words, you are welcome to join us! You will need to accomplish the following prior to departure:
​
  1. You need a current, up-to-date passport.

  2. You need to be in good physical health and have your immunizations are up-to-date.

  3. You need to complete an application (available under FORMS) and commit to pay or raise the trip costs  (ranging from $1100-1800 depending on airfare). More detailed information about what to expect on the installation can be found on the application and here.

  4. Prior to departure you will receive information about what to expect on the trip, including packing suggestions. Make sure to read and follow the directions carefully.  If you live in the Visalia area you may be invited to a team meeting in preparation for the trip. 

  5. It is most important that you make the decision to:
  • Have fun
  • Be flexible
  • Have a positive attitude
  • Be willing to submit to the leadership of the team
  • Participate in team nightly devotions
  • Adhere to the customs and culture of the country you visit
  • Drink water that comes from a sealed bottle only
  • Refrain from eating food prepared by street vendors
  • Be on time for all meetings and departure times to the installation site
