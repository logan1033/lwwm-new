OUR SYSTEM

OUR SYSTEM:

Designed for cost efficiency and ease of maintinance our system cleans water in stages. Scroll down and follow the waters path!

Operating Costs Include 3 years maintinance. ===>

(Parts and Labor!)

Basic Stats:

Methods: Filtration -> Microfiltration -> UV Disinfection -> Ozone Treatment

Capacity: Based on Tank Size. Roughly 300 gallons/hour @ 5gpm per batch.

COST: $4250 (Operational Costs/100,000 gallons = ~$0.0075/gallon)

Next

FILTRATION

The Sediment Filters

STEP 1 + 1.5

This is the Large Blue Cylinder in the photo and the smaller Clear Filter to it's side. They are the first step to clean water.

This step Filters out large items such as:

  • Sticks
  • Leaves
  • Soil
  • Anything bigger than ~30 microns

This Large Blue Filter removes the largest items of particulate matter from water and cleans it enough to continue to the next step.

The next step is the Clear Filter. This catches other things that could damage the pump.


Next

MICROFILTRATION

The 5-Micron & 0.5-Micron Filters

STEP 2 + 2.5

These are the First Two Blue Filters in the photo if you follow the pipes. (Nearest the black ring-tool.)

This step Filters out most illness causing pathogens such as:

  • Bacteria
  • Viruses
  • Cysts

This 5-Micron Filter removes many of the larger pathogens as well as visible particulates that would clog the 0.5 Filter quickly. The importance of visibility comes in Step 3!

The 0.5-Micron Filter get almost everything else. This removes ~90% of the harmful pathogens.

Next

UV DISINFECTION

The Ultraviolet Water Filter

STEP 3

This is the Third Blue Cylinder. (Near the white ring-tool.)

This step shines a harmful wavelength of light through water to eradicate living organisms.

Remember the importance of visibility? Step 2 removes things that would block the light.

This Step is NEW! (-ish) This step was added during COVID-19 to accomodate on-demand systems in hospitals rather than our previous batch process systems.

Our old systems are being updated over-time and all new systems are on-demand.

Next

OZONE TREATMENT

The Ozone Generator

STEP 4

This is the White Rectangle Box with Our Logo.

This step circulates ozone with the water to kill any and all remaining living organisms.

This the the process from which we get our LOGO!

The verticle section of pipes is called the 'churn' (Because that's what it does!) Ozone needs 30 vertical feet of pipe to be fully mixed with the water.

And that's IT! The water is now clean.
